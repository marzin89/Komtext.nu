Hi and welcome! I am a professional translator translating into Swedish and German. My source languages
are German, Swedish, English and French. Beyond this, I also offer proofreading of Swedish texts.

Why Komtext?

The name Komtext is a portmanteau of “Kommunikation” (Swedish/German spelling) and “text”. I believe
that translation and proofreading are ways of enabling and perfecting communication within and across
linguistic borders. Therefore, my aim is to deliver texts that reach out in the best way possible, according
to your needs and expectations. How can I help you? To learn more, please go to Translation and
proofreading.
